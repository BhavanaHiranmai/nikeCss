Nike Air Max

The Nike Air Max is a line of shoes known for its visible Air cushioning unit, offering comfort, style, and innovative design. Since its introduction, Air Max has become one of Nikeâ€™s most recognizable and influential sneaker collections.

History

The first Air Max, the Air Max 1, was released in 1987. Designed by Tinker Hatfield, it introduced the world to visible Air technology and transformed sneaker design forever. Many classic models followed, such as the Air Max 90, Air Max 95, and Air Max 97.

Design Features

Air Max shoes are known for their foam midsoles, visible Air units, sleek shapes, and bold colorways. Each model brings a unique evolution of style and performance.

Popularity

Air Max sneakers are worn worldwide for sports, fashion, and everyday comfort. Air Max Day, celebrated on March 26, highlights their cultural impact and ongoing legacy in the sneaker community.
